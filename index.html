Here’s how the game works.

The surrealists invented this at a dinner party in Paris, 1925. It was designed to produce absurd results by preventing collaboration. They thought that was funny. They were right.

01
Draw the head

One person draws a head at the top. Any head they like. A very important finance head. With compliance antennae.

→
02
Fold the paper

They fold the paper down so only the neck lines are visible. Then pass it on. No peeking. This is the important part.

→
03
Pass it on

The next person adds a body. The one after that adds legs. Nobody sees the whole thing. The game requires this.

→
04
Unfold it

You open the paper and look at what you made together. The result is always a monster. That’s the whole game.
